Our Mission

It is inevitable that with easy access to internet misinformation will be present. Specifically medical misinformation. This is why we decided to create TMM, a project dedicated to Tackling Medical Misinformation.

TMM is a website where you can input website URL's regarding anything related to medicine and we will grade it's reliability.

We acknowledge possible innacuracies with our service, feel free to get in contact with us at TacklingMedicalMisinformation@gmail.com
